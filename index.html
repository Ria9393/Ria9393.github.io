About Me

I am an Indian postgraduate research student, currently based in USA. I completed my Bachelors and Masters in Economics from Jadavpur University, Kolkata. I work as a Teaching Assistant in the Department of Economics at SUNY Buffalo.

Research Interests

Migration and Development Economics: Studying factors that influence cross-country migration, investigating effect of migrant’s choices and behavior on factors that influence development outcomes, over time.
Migration and cross-country inequality: Comparing countries on the basis of effects of migration on development outcomes.
Policy development: Investigating and developing cross-country short-term and long-term migration policies.
